What We Make

We produce the following types of packaging. Click any product to see more details.

Jewellery Stand-Up Pouch

Plastic pouch with zip lock. Used for packing earrings, rings, bangles, and sets. Custom printed with your brand name.

  • Zip lock closure
  • Matte or glossy finish
  • Custom size and print
  • Min. order: 5,000 pcs

Jewellery Blister Pack

Plastic blister with card backing. Used for earrings, pendants, rings in retail shops. Clear plastic front, printed card back.

  • Clear PVC / PET front
  • Printed card backing
  • Custom cavity shape
  • Min. order: 2,000 pcs

Flat / Pillow Pouch

Simple flat sealed pouch. Suitable for small accessories, hair pins, clips, and similar items. Available with or without print.

  • Heat sealed
  • Plain or printed
  • Multiple sizes
  • Min. order: 10,000 pcs

Clamshell Blister Pack

Hinged plastic box — opens and closes. Used for bangles, necklace sets, and gift items in retail display.

  • Hinged lid
  • Clear PET plastic
  • Custom shape / size
  • Min. order: 2,000 pcs

Mobile Cover Pouch

Clear zip-lock pouch for packing mobile covers, phone accessories, and electronics retail packaging.

  • Crystal clear PVC
  • Zip-lock closure
  • Custom size
  • Min. order: 5,000 pcs
